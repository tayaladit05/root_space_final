Agent Ownership:
Share in our
success

At FIND, we believe our agents are the heart of our brokerage, and their contributions should be recognized in meaningful ways. That’s why we proudly operate as an agent-owned brokerage, offering all eligible agents the opportunity to become owners.

This ownership model is designed to empower you, giving you a direct stake in the company’s success from the moment you meet the qualifications. By aligning your growth with FIND’s, we create a shared purpose: delivering exceptional client experiences while building a thriving, innovative brokerage.

As an owner, you’ll enjoy a voice in shaping the direction of our company, and the pride that comes with being more than just an agent—you’re a key stakeholder in FIND’s success.

At FIND, your achievements don’t just advance your career; they contribute to the future of a brokerage where every agent has the chance to share in the rewards of their hard work.
